Month 9:Readiness & Meeting

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate: Vitamin K & Small Meals. Vitamin K (found in Swiss chard and cauliflower) helps with blood clotting after birth. Since your stomach is compressed, switch to 5 or 6 small meals a day instead of 3 large ones.

🧘 Exercise

Focus on deep breathing and gentle stretching. Short walks and "Tailor Sitting" (sitting cross-legged) can help open the pelvis for delivery.
