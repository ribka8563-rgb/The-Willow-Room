Month 2:The Heartbeat and Energy

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate: Ginger & Magnesium. If you have nausea, try ginger tea, bananas, and crackers. Magnesium-rich foods like almonds and pumpkin seeds can also help with early pregnancy fatigue..

🧘 Exercise

If you were active before pregnancy, you can usually continue your normal routine with slight intensity adjustments. If you are new to exercise, start with 5–10 minutes a day and work up to 30 minutes.
