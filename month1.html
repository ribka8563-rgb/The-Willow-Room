Month 1: The Beginning

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate: Folic Acid & B12. Focus on leafy greens (spinach, kale), beans, and fortified cereals. This helps prevent birth defects of the brain and spine.

🧘 Exercise

Gentle 15-minute walks to keep blood flowing.Continue your usual routine but prioritize hydration. If you weren't active before, start with 15-minute brisk walks.
