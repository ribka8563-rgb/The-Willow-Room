Month 4:Movement & Comfort

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate:Iron-Rich Foods. Your baby is making their own blood now. Lean red meat, lentils, tofu, and beans are essential to prevent anemia..

🧘 Exercise

Avoid exercising flat on your back for long periods, as the weight of the uterus can compress a major vein (the vena cava). Use a wedge pillow for floor work.
