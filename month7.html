Month 7 :Preparation & Strength

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate:Protein Power. The baby is putting on weight quickly. Focus on eggs, Greek yogurt, chicken, and lean proteins to support muscle growth.

🧘 Exercise

Water-based exercises (Aquanatal classes) are ideal now because the water supports 90% of your body weight, relieving pressure on your lower back.
