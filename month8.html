Month 8:Patience & Position

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate:Fiber & Slow Carbs. Digestion slows down, which can lead to heartburn or constipation. Eat high-fiber foods like oats, brown rice, and berries to keep things moving.

🧘 Exercise

Use a birthing ball (stability ball) for seated stretches. Bouncing gently or rotating your hips on the ball can help with pelvic alignment.
