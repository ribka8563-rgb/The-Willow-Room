Month 6:The Glow & Balance

🍎 What to Eat

Focus on Folate:Omega-3 Fatty Acids (DHA). This month is critical for the baby’s brain and eye development. Eat low-mercury fish (like salmon), walnuts, or chia seeds.

🧘 Exercise

Focus on strength training with lighter weights. Stronger back and shoulder muscles will help prevent the "pregnancy slouch" and prepare you for carrying the baby.
